• List all the products from the catalog : localhost:8080/products
  • See the product's detail : localhost:8080/product/{id} , {id} has to be replace by the product id you want
  • List the items of your cart : localhost:8080/cart
  • Add a product to your cart : localhost:8080/add/{id} , {id} has to be replace by the product id you want
  • Remove a product from your cart : localhost:8080/remove/{id} , {id} has to be replace by the product id you want
